Vectors

Definition

  • Specify a direction or point in \(n\) dimensional space

2D

  • \(\boldsymbol{i}\) is the unit vector along the x axis
  • \(\boldsymbol{j}\) is the unit vector along the y axis

Representation

  • \(2\boldsymbol{i} + 3\boldsymbol{j}\)
  • \(\binom{2}{3}\)
