Differentiation

Polynomials

Rule

$$ \begin{align*} y & = ax^n \\ \frac{dy}{dx} & = anx^{n - 1} \\ \end{align*} $$

Common Derivatives

  • \(y = x^2\), \(y' = 2x\)
  • \(y = \frac{a}{x}\), \(y' = -\frac{a}{x^2}\)
  • \(y = \frac{a}{x^n}\), \(y' = -\frac{na}{x^{n + 1}}\)
  • \(y = a\sqrt{x}\), \(y' = \frac{a}{2\sqrt{x}}\)

Product Rule

$$ y = uv \\ \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{du}{dx} v + \frac{dv}{dx} u \\ $$

Quotient Rule

$$ y = \frac{u}{v} \\ \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{\frac{du}{dx} v - \frac{dv}{dx} u}{v^2} \\ $$

Chain Rule

$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{dy}{du} \frac{du}{dx} $$

Trigonometry

  • \(y = \sin{x}\), \(y' = \cos{x}\)
  • \(y = \cos{x}\), \(y' = -\sin{x}\)
  • \(y = \tan{x}\), \(y' = \frac{1}{\cos^2{x}}\)
